Support Our Marching Band!

Thank you for supporting Freshman clarinet player Ethan and the entire Moon Valley High School Marching Band! In this fundraiser, you can "claim" a day from the calendar above by donating the corresponding dollar amount (e.g., claiming the 17th is a $17 donation).

Your contribution helps cover essential costs like new instruments, uniforms, and travel expenses. While the main goal is to support the band, Ethan has a personal challenge to get all 31 days on his chart filled to earn a special prize!

Ready to donate? Click here, then please remember to manually enter the dollar amount for the day you chose and enter Ethan's name so he gets credit!
